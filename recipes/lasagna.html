Lasagna Recipe

Description Of Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato passata
  • Lasagna noddles
  • Seasonings
  • Bechamel sauce
    • Flour
    • Butter
    • Milk
    • Cheese
    • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna by:
    1. Noodles
    2. Meat sauce
    3. Bechamel sauce
    4. Repeat
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the Lasagna rest before serving
